A New “Millenium City Center” to Be
Built in Budapest
MTI-Hungary Today (https://hungarytoday.hu/author/mti-hungary-today/) 2023.12.05.
A new “millennium city center” will be created in the area of the
Rákosrendező railway station, on an area of about 130 hectares (1.3 million
square meters) that will elevate Budapest to the ranks of modern world
cities, Minister of Construction and Transport János Lázár announced at a
press conference on Monday.
