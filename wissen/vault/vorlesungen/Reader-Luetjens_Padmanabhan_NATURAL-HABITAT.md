# Reader Lütjens_Padmanabhan_NATURAL HABITAT

## S. 1

There is a deep divide between our scientific and our romantic 
understanding of Nature.
Architectural form was considered an integral part of the natural 
world. In the 18th century, with the dawn of romanticism, we 
began to separate our sensual perception of nature from its 
scientific understanding. With the advent of the age of the 
Anthropocene, that opposition has become obsolete.
 
In our design studio, NATURAL HABITAT we explore ideas for a 
new union of natural and architectonic form. The students work 
on the transformation of a cooperative housing estate and its 
human-made landscape.
