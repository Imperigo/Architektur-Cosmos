## S. 2

Unseen Cosmic Forces
Technique:
Bringing Cai’s art style technique from 2D to 
3D.
Atmosphere:
Photography of a captured moment in time, 
stepping into the impact.
Lighting with Blackpowder:
Interior light is guided by the blackpowder 
paintings, optimized to enhance the artwork.
Entering:
Being introduced to unseen cosmic forces 
through a wormhole in another dimension, 
building tension and curiosity with every step 
forward.
The Flow:
The eye traces back to the original point of 
impact, guided naturally by the walls.
Materials:
Using quartz sand and glass as 
countermaterials to Blackpowder.
