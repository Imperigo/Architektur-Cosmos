We studied the 3 options and realized that the three lead to 
inefficient use of material due to the fact there is too much 
difference between the largest and shortest spans. A more 
homogeneous raster could solve this problem. 
Looking at the floorplan, we realize that the day-zone, a space of 
7x12 meters, is anyways too wide. If we introduce a balcony, we 
can bring inwards the line of colums in the fassade, reducing the 
largest spans and obtaining a more homogeneous raster.
