experiments.

shows us clearly, that the ratio between Room and Explosion is not comparable
to the Work of Cai!.

For the first time we create a line of explosion. This
should emulate the Art work's explosion better.
And also changing the amount of grams to see how
the reaction to the wall changes.
Results showed that 3g was to less to impact on the
wall. Ea05-02 with 6g was almost too much. But it
showed results on the wall.
What is clearly visible is how the smoke escaped in
the ceiling and how it painted the walls with it.
Traces go from light gray to dark gray from the inside
to outside which shows the initial blow and the after
burn which takes the sooth outside the box.
