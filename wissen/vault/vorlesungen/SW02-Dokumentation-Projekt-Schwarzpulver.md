## S. 5

Trial and Error 01
24.09.2024
expanded Polystyrene
As our first attempt on igniting fireworks on diffe­
rent surfaces we chose the expanded Polystyrene. 
Polystyrene is easily flammable. The results showed 
us what we expectet. The heat of the Firework mel­
ted the Styrofoam where the fire was.
It left behind a 3-dimensional hole and grime tra­
ces of the ignition.
The different behavior of the explosives affected 
the form of the melted Styrofoam and led to a con­
trollable result.

Trial and Error 01
24.09.2024
Sand-lime Brick
In difference to the Styrofoam, the Brick is fire­
proof and much more dense which concludes in a 
unpenetrated Brick-Surface.
It is possible to reconstruct the type of firework 
even after it exploded. The grim shows where the 
starting ignition was and how the explosion beha­
ved. As an example;
The Fuse and Lady Cracker uses a String to connect 
smaller ignitions or is the ignition itself. It shows 
the controllability of the direction of the String.
In comparison to the Meteorites and the Fire Ring 
which once they‘re ignited, will be uncontrollable.
