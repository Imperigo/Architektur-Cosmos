## S. 27

27
THG Emissionen
Einführung
Unsere Empfehlungen – Keine negative Emissionen
No negative emissions from 
biogenic carbon. List 
biogenic carbon separate from 
upfront (embodied) emissions.
No credits (negative emissions) 
for grid exports. Reduce the 
embodied emissions of PV 
proportional to self-consumption 
(pro-rata approach).
For further details see: Accounting Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Emissions in Building Design: 
A White Paper on the GHG Emissions Timeline (GET), 2022, V. Piccioni, L. Walker, E. 
Borkowski, I. Hischier, A. Schlueter, https://systems.arch.ethz.ch/publications
