## S. 26

SPATIAL SYNERGIES
2.3 Urban development of the work and residential zone Telli Ost, 
City of Aarau
-
The dialog-based process with the owners, planners, experts and the city of 
Aarau enabled the development of three very exciting project approaches, 
which show different solutions for the future development.
-
The test planning made it possible to involve the local population in the 
desired site transformation at an early stage, and the response to the 
development was very positive.
-
Convincing development concepts were submitted, although it also became 
clear that due to the complexity of the task (potential for high building density, 
many specifications and requirements as well as a long-term planning horizon), 
no project with the level of detail for a guiding project project (Richtprojekt) was 
yet provided.
-
The steering committee proposed the project of the first team for further 
elaboration in the context of a guiding project (Richtprojekt) as a basis for the 
Guiding development plan (Entwicklungsrichtplan) (legal-binding instrument).
