## S. 2

Title of the Work
Project descriptions and other short texts 
always start top left, set in single column. 
Emphasis typically denoted with italics is 
indicated using ‘single quotes.’
Paragraphs are always separated by single 
blank line.
In this studio we focus on the unique gestures 
dictated by a particular material and the 
specialisms of working such material, in what 
we call ‘material gesture’. We will explore 
the making in relation to a material, in order 
to produce an architecture that is solely 
focused on the relationship between the two. 
Our interests in the material are linked to its 
properties, mining, industry, craftsmanship, 
specialised fabrication techniques, cultural 
significance and innovation in the field of 
architecture.
 
The research that is carried out in the studio 
is largely developed through a practice based 
approach, applied throughout the intrinsic 
developments of the projects in the design 
studio. Each semester a subtopic within 
material gesture will be explored: ranging 
from an in-depth focus on one specific source 
material: gypsum, to the geological interest of 
a site, and the man-made materials from our 
current geological age: the Anthropocene.
The results will be collected in a growing 
physical database of materials and its 
performance through its applications in the 
form of large architectural models, made in the 
actual researched materials and techniques.
Thank you.

## S. 10

Page layout & typography
• Format A2 (420 × 594 mm).
• Margin for image layout 20 mm; 41 mm at the bottom to leave space for footer and pagination when 
compiled into a publication.
• Margin for text layout 23 mm left & right edge; 20 mm top; 41 mm bottom.
• Typeface Agipo Bold Condensed (radimpesko.com). Please note Studio Anne Holtrop at ETH Zürich 
licensed the font only for a limited number of computers. You are granted to use the font while 
participating in the studio, and are kindly ask to remove the font from your computer immediately 
afterwards in order to prevent breach of the font EULA (licensing agreement).
• Font-size 25,5 pt, line-spacing (leading) 28,5 pt, tracking (spacing between individual characters) 25.
• All text is left-aligned, no hyphenation, everything in single (abovementioned) font size, always 
Sentence case, never UPPERCASE.

## S. 12

Images
• Please make sure the images are hi-res, at least 300 dpi.
• By default, all images are aligned with top margin of the page. However, you are free to center or 
align them at the bottom, if that helps the composition of your pages.
• Prefer one image per page, unless combination of two or more conveys a meaning.
• Horizontal spacing between images is in multiplies of 10 mm, 
vertical follows the baseline grid of 28,5 pt.
• No need to completely fill each page, white space is welcome!

## S. 14

Captions, legends & project descriptions
• Captions always sit just below the image they describe. Typically they are full sentences and 
therefore end with a punctuation.
• Legends are left-aligned, placed preferrably on top or bottom of a page.
