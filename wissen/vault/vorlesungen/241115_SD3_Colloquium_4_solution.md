## S. 7

The center of mass of a structure is the point at which the 
entire mass of the structure can be considered to be 
concentrated.
The center of stiffness is the point in a structure that 
averages (balances) the stiffness of all bracing elements. 
It is the point where if we would apply a lateral load the 
structure would only translate without rotation.
