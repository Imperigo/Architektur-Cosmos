This series represents an airtight room which has only one Exhaust.
Because of the airtightness the Blackpowder-Fire happens way faster an created
a small explosion inside the box
In Comparison to the other Experiments, this Explosion is shorter but with more
energy.
It differs itself from the others at the ceiling. The fire is exiting through the
Ignition-air-hole. The ceiling is therefore not as strongly deformed as the other
experiments.
The height difference results shows that sooth is visible till the height of 3.5m.
Comparing the Explosion Size of 3g compared to the 3x3m Space of the Box
shows us clearly, that the ratio between Room and Explosion is not comparable
to the Work of Cai!.
