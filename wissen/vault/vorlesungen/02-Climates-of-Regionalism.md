5. Core Theoretical Questions
• Architecture and climate:
• Should not be reduced to shelter.
• Climate must be integrated into design, aesthetics, and culture.
• Regionalism is not simply about formal style; it involves:
• Local conditions (materials, energy, environment).
• Sustainability.
• Lifestyle and cultural meaning.
• Tension remains between practical adaptation and formal expression (climate as function vs. climate as art).
