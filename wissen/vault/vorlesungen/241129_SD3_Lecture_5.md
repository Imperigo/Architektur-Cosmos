TEIL A
Hochhäuser und Spezialfälle
“Every structural type, whether an organism or artifact, has a maximum and minimum
size. In the tall building there are two limitations on height; one is the structural and the
other the functional. When a certain magnitude has been reached in the tall building the
structural system must be changed. A new structural system gives the possibility of a
new architectural expression”
- Myron Goldsmith
