# blackpowder Experimente

## S. 1

Our newest addition to our fireworks is Blackpow­
der. Which should help us to get closer to the 
Artwork of Cai.
For precaution we used a small dose of Blackpow­
der to get a feeling for it‘s use. As a base to ignite 
the powder, we used a cement board. It breaks 
easier than normal stone and the surface is way 
more editable.
The result was astonishing. For the first time we 
saw a glimpse of the power of Blackpowder. The 
heat created Bubbles and small craters in the ce­
ment. Right in the mix was also traces of soot.

## S. 3

Now we are ready for our first experiment with a 
large mass of black powder. To do this, we have 
created a safe environment around it. We prepa­
red water and flannels for small fires and the fire 
extinguisher for an emergency situation.
It created a large ball of fire. No explosion.
Lots of hot sprinkles from the fireball.
It left little craters behind and the color of the 
cement changed. It looks like the heat has added 
a new layer onto the cement.
